Steigenberger Hotel El Lessan

Located in Ras El Bar, Al Lisan Steinberger Hotel offers a bar and a terrace. This 5-star hotel also has a restaurant and air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi. They all have a private bathroom, in addition to an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness center, a 24-hour front desk and evening entertainment events.

Raw materials for the project

The control of the product is carried out through the first steps to selecting the excellent quality materials.

The materials are selected according to international standards and the requirements of the Egyptian Code, and the largest and best international companies are dealt with to supply raw materials in Egypt.

  • Cement : From Arab Cement Company - CEMEX - Lafarge - El Arish - Beni Suef - Sinai
  • Additions : From Sika Company - BASF - AIMC - Chemicals for Modern Building
  • Rubble : From Ataqa Company (Al-Daly - Egyptian - Al-Salam)

One of the branches of Al-Muddathir Group of Companies for Trade, Investment and Contracting.

One of the leading companies in the field of ready-mixed concrete in the Egyptian market.

It was established in 2015 with the aim of meeting all the needs of its customers from concrete, to be an essential partner in the supply and production of concrete and providing advanced and sustainable solutions for the ready-mixed concrete industry in Egypt.

The company started establishing its first plant in the East Delta in Damietta, and within two years the company expanded in (Cairo - Port Said - New Mansoura) committed to international standards of production and quality control of ready-mixed concrete.
